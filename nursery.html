Nursery Program

4.5 – 5.5 years | School-readiness with strong academic foundation

About Our Nursery Program

Our Nursery Program focuses on school-readiness with a strong academic foundation and socio-emotional growth. Designed for children aged 4.5 to 5.5 years, this program prepares children for the transition to formal schooling while maintaining the child-centered Montessori approach.

Children develop advanced academic skills, critical thinking abilities, and the confidence needed for future educational success, all while fostering independence, creativity, and a lifelong love of learning.

Program Highlights

Reading & Writing

Advanced literacy skills including fluent reading, creative writing, and comprehensive language arts instruction.

Math Basics

Solid mathematical foundation with arithmetic operations, problem-solving, and logical thinking development.

Confidence & Care

Building self-confidence, empathy, and caring relationships while developing leadership and social responsibility.
