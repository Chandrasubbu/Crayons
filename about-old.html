About Crayons Montessori

Nurturing young minds with love, care, and excellence since 2005

Our Story

Founded in 2005 in the heart of Ranchi, Jharkhand, Crayons Montessori began with a simple yet profound vision: to create a nurturing environment where every child could discover their unique potential and develop a lifelong love for learning.

For 19 years, we have been dedicated to providing authentic Montessori education that respects the natural development of children. Our journey started with just a handful of children and has grown into a thriving community of young learners, passionate educators, and supportive families.

What sets us apart is our unwavering commitment to the Montessori philosophy - we believe that children are naturally eager to learn and capable of initiating learning in a supportive, thoughtfully prepared environment.

Our Mission

To provide a carefully prepared Montessori environment that fosters independence, creativity, and a love for learning in every child. We are committed to nurturing confident, compassionate, and capable individuals who will contribute positively to society.

Our Vision

To be the leading Montessori institution in Ranchi, recognized for our excellence in early childhood education, innovative teaching methods, and our commitment to developing well-rounded individuals who are prepared for future academic and life success.

Our Core Values

Respect

We respect each child as a unique individual with their own learning pace and style.

Growth

We foster holistic development - intellectual, emotional, social, and physical.

Community

We build strong partnerships with families and create a supportive learning community.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in everything we do, from teaching to facilities to care.

Innovation

Safety

We provide a safe, secure, and nurturing environment for all our children.
